Sobre nosotros

KQ Interiores es un estudio de diseño que crea interiores de alta calidad con base en Punta del Este, Uruguay.

Liderado por Lucía Arreseigor y Joaquín Melogno (…) providenciamos un servicio de diseño personalizado para crear interiores que se ajusten perfectamente a las necesidades del cliente y que perduren en el tiempo. Apuntamos principalmente al confort, sofisticación y practicidad.

Nuestros proveedores son cuidadosamente seleccionados para cerciorarnos que son adeptos para llevar a cabo nuestros diseños en sintonía con el nivel de precisión, calidad y cuidado con el que trabajamos.

Nuestro foco está en ofrecer un servicio de diseño altamente personalizado que fusione los deseos y los sueños de nuestros clientes.

Somos conscientes que el éxito de nuestros proyectos
no solo recae en el resultado final, sino que, en todo el proceso,
y estamos orgullosos del nivel de servicio que ofrecemos a nuestros clientes, dedicándoles el tiempo y la atención que merecen.

Nuestros servicios : residenciales y comerciales

Nuestra pasión es crear atemporales, bellos y confortables interiores. A la hora de diseñar, tenemos en cuenta los requerimientos específicos de cada cliente.

A lo largo de nuestros años de experiencia hemos perfeccionado nuestro servicio, asegurándonos de providenciar un servicio integral de diseño de interiores que se divide en las siguientes etapas:

The Brief

Key to the success of any project is ensuring that the interiors are an authentic reflection of our client’s requirements and aspirations. During the brief stage we take the time to learn about how the client... wishes to use the property, their personal style, lifestyle and aspirations. We will build a picture of the project through a site visit or floor plans and photographs as well as ensure that we understand all factors such as time scale, budget, and any structural or administrative requirements. Following this thorough briefing stage we will send a proposal to the client outlining the brief as we understand it, with an estimated budget for the FF&E and our associated design fee.
READ MORE

Concept

Interpreting the detailed brief we will present the concept designs, consisting of floor plans illustrating the proposed furniture layouts and any structural changes that may be required, and mood board presentations containing samples of all proposed... finishes as well as imagery of suggested furniture and joinery concepts. This is a very collaborative stage and we invite our clients to give as much feedback as possible to ensure we are on the right page.
READ MORE

Detail

Having approved all of the concept designs we progress to the design development and detail design stage. All final bespoke furniture, joinery and interior architecture drawings will be presented, along with a all final finishes and... curated art and accessories for clients to approve. At this stage we will also present a fully itemised breakdown of costs in line with the agreed budget.
READ MORE

Realisation

The schedule of works will be outlined and agreed. With all onsite requirements met, works will commence and our project coordinators and designers will attend regular site meetings to ensure our designs are being carried out... to their exact specifications and that the project is progressing as planned. We will keep the client updated throughout as well as oversee the snagging during the snagging period, allowing our client to enjoy their property with peace of mind.
